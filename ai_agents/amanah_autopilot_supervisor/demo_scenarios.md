# Amanah Autopilot Supervisor — Demo Scenarios

## Investor demo scenario

**Audience:** investor asking "what happens between site visits — is anyone
watching the portfolio overnight?"

Amanah Autopilot Supervisor's morning briefing shows exactly what changed
overnight across the portfolio — 4 projects newly finance-ready, 2 blocked by
consent, 1 MRV badge blocked by missing after-data — with every item routed
to a human approval queue, never auto-resolved.

**Why it lands:** it shows 24/7 portfolio oversight without pretending the
system makes decisions on its own — machine speed, human judgement.

## Enterprise / government use case

**Audience:** an operations team managing dozens of sites who cannot
manually check every project daily.

The morning briefing becomes the operations team's daily starting point — a
prioritised, specific list of what needs a human decision today, instead of
each project requiring an ad hoc status check.

## Islamic ethical finance / Amanah alignment

The name itself reflects the design: Amanah (trust, safekeeping) means this
supervisor holds every gap and risk in trust for a human decision-maker,
never exercising independent authority over high-impact outcomes — a direct
translation of the ethical principle into the agent's operating rules.

## MRV / audit trail requirements

Every overnight finding is timestamped and linked to the specific project/
evidence gap it identified, creating a running audit log of what was found,
when, and what human decision followed.
