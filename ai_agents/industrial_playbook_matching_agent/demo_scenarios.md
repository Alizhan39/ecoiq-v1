# Industrial Playbook Matching Agent — Demo Scenarios

## Investor demo scenario

**Audience:** development bank comparing multiple industrial retrofit
opportunities across a country portfolio.

Industrial Playbook Matching Agent shows, for each asset, exactly which
playbook fits and why — with quick wins separated from deep upgrades — so
the investor can prioritise fast, well-evidenced wins first.

## Enterprise / government use case

**Audience:** a ministry of industry standardising retrofit prioritisation
across a national portfolio of factories and municipal boiler houses.

Running this agent across hundreds of Asset Passports produces a comparable,
evidence-anchored shortlist rather than ad hoc site visits deciding priority
one at a time.

## Islamic ethical finance / Amanah alignment

No Harm risks (safety exposure, community impact during works) are surfaced
explicitly for every playbook match, supporting a Maqasid-aligned review
before capital is committed.

## MRV / audit trail requirements

The `mrv_metrics` field tells MRV Agent exactly what to measure before/after
implementation — so MRV readiness is designed in from the playbook match
stage, not bolted on afterwards.
