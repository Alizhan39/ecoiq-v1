# Governance Agent — Demo Scenarios

## Investor demo scenario

**Audience:** board member asking "who actually signed off on this claim?"

Governance Agent shows the exact reviewer type routed, the specific blockers
that existed and were resolved, and confirms the approval status reflects
a real recorded decision — not an automated rubber stamp.

## Enterprise / government use case

**Audience:** a government compliance office auditing how public claims get
approved before publication.

Governance Agent's packet structure gives auditors a consistent, traceable
approval record across every project — reviewer type, checklist, blockers,
and decision — rather than ad hoc email approvals.

## Islamic ethical finance / Amanah alignment

Every Maqasid/Mizan or Islamic finance packet is explicitly routed to a
qualified reviewer type — Governance Agent never resolves the ethical
judgement itself, keeping the human-in-the-loop principle intact.

## MRV / audit trail requirements

The review packet is itself part of the audit trail: it records which
reviewer type saw which evidence, and preserves the blockers that existed
before approval — a full governance record, not just a final "approved" stamp.
