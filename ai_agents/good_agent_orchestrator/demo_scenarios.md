## Investor demo scenario

1. A signal arrives: "High household heating costs and coal-smoke
   pollution across ~200 homes in the Almaty region."
2. The deterministic Layer 1-3 filter activates 6 seeded principle lenses
   (energy transition, vulnerable-groups protection, ecological
   stewardship, accountability, equitable access, energy-as-shared-resource)
   out of the full 114 — never all of them.
3. One combined Layer 4 reasoning call returns a position per lens. The
   equitable-access lens raises a real affordability concern — this is
   preserved, not averaged into a fake consensus.
4. The resulting `GoodOpportunity` feeds the existing Better Way / Capital
   Guardian pipeline unchanged, producing a ranked intervention comparison,
   a governed `CapitalAllocationDecision`, and an `ImpactReceipt` with an
   MRV plan.

Run it yourself: `python manage.py run_almaty_good_agent_demo`.
