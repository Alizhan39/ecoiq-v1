# Research Agent — Demo Scenarios

## Investor demo scenario

**Audience:** impact investor evaluating a Kazakhstan district heating
portfolio.

1. Investor asks: "What do we know publicly about this district heating
   operator before we send a technical team?"
2. Research Agent pulls the company's public disclosures, national energy
   ministry filings, and news coverage of prior modernisation attempts.
3. Output shows: a source-linked evidence summary, a confidence level
   (medium — some fields missing), and an explicit "missing_data" list (e.g.
   "no independently audited emissions baseline found").
4. Investor sees exactly what is known, what is not, and where the trail
   leads next (Document Reader Agent once real project documents arrive).

**Why it lands:** it demonstrates EcoIQ does not fabricate confidence — it
shows investors the actual evidence boundary, which builds trust faster than
a polished but unsupported summary would.

## Enterprise / government use case

**Audience:** an akimat (municipal government) evaluating which local
industrial operators to prioritise for a clean-heating retrofit programme.

1. Government partner supplies a list of 10 candidate companies/operators.
2. Research Agent runs public research on each: energy use disclosures, prior
   modernisation announcements, ownership structure.
3. Output feeds the **Country Transition Atlas** and **Portfolio & Country
   Transition Atlas** cluster view, helping the government see which
   operators already have public sustainability commitments to build on.

## Islamic ethical finance / Amanah alignment

Where a company has made public statements about Islamic finance
compatibility, ethical sourcing, or waqf/sadaqah-linked programmes, Research
Agent surfaces these as **reported claims** with their source — never as an
EcoIQ certification. Maqasid/Mizan alignment scoring is a separate,
human-reviewed process (see the Certification & Trust Badge Engine and
Governance Agent).
