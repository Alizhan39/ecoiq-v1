How It Works

From audit documents
to action plan — in minutes.

Two sequential AI calls transform raw facility data into a board-ready modernization report with quantified findings and phased ROI roadmap.

01

Upload Industrial Audit

Upload facility documents — energy reports, maintenance logs, P&IDs, environmental permits. Describe the site profile and complete a structured operations questionnaire.

02

AI Diagnostic Analysis

EcoIQ's AI analyses the facility across energy, maintenance, production, safety, and sustainability vectors — surfacing specific findings with financial quantification and root-cause attribution.

03

ROI + Modernization Roadmap

Receive a phased 36-month implementation roadmap with priority-scored recommendations, illustrative ROI estimates, before/after projections, and sustainability impact per intervention.

Financial estimates and KPI projections produced by EcoIQ are AI-assisted and illustrative in nature. All figures should be validated by qualified engineers before capital commitment.
