EcoIQ Amanah Autopilot

AI agents that do good while you sleep.

EcoIQ runs ethical AI agents overnight to detect waste, harm and imbalance across energy, industry, cities and communities. While users sleep, the system finds modernisation opportunities, prepares funding routes, drafts reports and tracks verified impact — aligned with Maqasid, Mizan and amanah.

Maqasid and Mizan are used here as an ethical decision-support framework, not a religious ruling or fatwa. Every finding is evidence-linked and requires human approval before any modernisation, funding, or supplier action is taken.
