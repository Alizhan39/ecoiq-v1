EcoIQ Mobile / iPad Inspection Mode

Capture evidence on site. Generate modernisation intelligence instantly.

EcoIQ Mobile / iPad Inspection Mode lets field teams inspect boilers, factories, farms, mines, buildings, heat networks and energy assets directly from a mobile device. Users can take photos, record videos, upload bills, capture meter readings, add voice notes and trigger AI agents that create Asset Passports, match playbooks, detect risks, prepare finance logic and start MRV tracking.
