{% trans "Our Mission" %}

Heavy industry — oil & gas, manufacturing, chemicals, metals — accounts for the majority of global emissions and employs hundreds of millions of people. These sectors cannot be simply shut down. They must be transformed.

EcoIQ was built to accelerate that transformation by making ethical performance visible, comparable, and actionable. We believe that transparency creates accountability, and that accountability creates change.

Our mission is to provide the most rigorous, publicly available ethical intelligence on industrial companies — so that capital flows to companies doing the hard work of genuine modernisation, not just those best at greenwashing.

{% trans "The EcoIQ Framework" %}

Every EcoIQ score is built from six dimensions, each grounded in a different aspect of long-term ethical value creation. The framework draws on principles of public stewardship, responsible governance, and evidence-based improvement that have proven durable across economies and cultures.

{% trans "Our Principles" %}

EcoIQ's design is guided by five commitments we hold to be non-negotiable, regardless of commercial pressure:

{% trans "Protect Life" %} We will not rate a company highly if it harms the health of workers, communities, or ecosystems. Harm signals reduce scores — there is no offset.
{% trans "Honest Evidence" %} Every score dimension is sourced from verifiable public information. We flag confidence levels and distinguish AI-generated analysis from verified data.
{% trans "Long-term Resilience" %} We reward companies building durable, sustainable businesses — not those optimising short-term profit at the expense of future generations.
{% trans "Public Benefit Priority" %} Companies that serve communities well, create quality jobs, and contribute to societal progress are rewarded. Narrow profit maximisation is insufficient.
{% trans "No Greenwashing" %} We penalise companies that make public commitments but fail to show evidence of action. Words without data do not improve scores.

{% trans "What EcoIQ Is Not" %}

We are not an ESG rating agency. Traditional ESG ratings serve financial disclosure. EcoIQ scores ethical performance and industrial transition readiness. These are related but distinct.

We are not a consultancy. We do not accept payment from companies to improve their scores. All scoring is conducted independently, based on publicly available evidence.

We are not infallible. EcoIQ scores are AI-assisted and indicative. They are built from public information, which is often incomplete. We display confidence levels and encourage companies to submit corrections through our profile claim process.
