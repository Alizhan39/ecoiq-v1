Generate investor, board, government and impact packs from verified evidence.

EcoIQ Executive Briefing & Board Pack Generator turns complex industrial modernisation data into clear decision-ready documents. It creates investor memos, board packs, akimat/government briefs, CSR sponsor packs, Islamic finance summaries, country transition briefs and verified impact reports using evidence from Asset Passports, Data Room, MRV, Finance Engine, Governance Review and Country Atlas.
