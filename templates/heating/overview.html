The Problem — coal heating in homes

Tens of thousands of private homes around Almaty heat with coal. In a mountain valley with winter temperature inversions, that household smoke is a leading driver of severe seasonal PM2.5 — and it means hauling fuel, daily stoking, dust, and fire and carbon-monoxide risk.

The Solution — an electric boiler retrofit kit

Khalifa Heat replaces the coal boiler with a correctly-sized electric boiler, wired safely (breakers + RCD/УЗО), with a thermostat and safety group, connected to existing radiators. No coal, no smoke, no daily stoking — installed in about a day.

Cheaper to start

Electric boilers cost a fraction of heat pumps and are easy to source and install locally in Kazakhstan.

Safe & standard

Every kit ships with proper electrical protection, cable specs and a clear manual. Optional professional install.

Heat-pump-ready

Choose Ready+ and your home is pre-built for a 3–4× more efficient heat-pump upgrade later.
