EcoIQ Public Trust & Impact Portal

Show verified impact publicly without exposing sensitive evidence.

EcoIQ Public Trust & Impact Portal turns approved MRV results into public-facing impact stories, country progress pages, sponsor reports and before/after summaries. It helps communities, donors, CSR sponsors, governments and investors see what changed, what harm was reduced and which projects have verified impact, while protecting confidential documents, personal data, financial records and sensitive industrial information.
