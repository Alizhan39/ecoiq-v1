EcoIQ Asset Passport

Every industrial asset becomes a measurable modernisation project.

EcoIQ Asset Passport combines photos, documents, sensor data, digital twin relationships, risk signals, Maqasid/Mizan scoring, finance readiness and verified impact into one living record. It helps factories, cities, investors and communities understand what an asset is, what harm or waste it creates, what should be modernised first and how impact will be proven.
