Methodology

From public evidence to climate intelligence

EcoIQ reads annual reports, sustainability disclosures, and regulatory filings — then applies a consistent 6-pillar framework to score and rank every company.

📄
Step 1
Evidence Ingestion

Annual reports, sustainability filings, regulatory disclosures, and public databases are ingested and indexed for each company.

🧠
Step 2
AI Analysis

Claude AI analyses each evidence source across six pillars — assigning scores, identifying risks, and flagging hidden harm signals.

📊
Step 3
Climate Score

A composite EcoIQ score (0–100) is calculated with weighted pillars and a harm penalty for pollution, controversy, and governance failures.

🗺
Step 4
Action Roadmap

Each company profile includes a personalised path-to-100 roadmap with financing options, modernisation priorities, and transition milestones.
