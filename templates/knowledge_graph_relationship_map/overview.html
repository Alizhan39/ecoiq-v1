EcoIQ Knowledge Graph & Relationship Map

Connect assets, evidence, risks, finance, suppliers and verified impact.

EcoIQ Knowledge Graph & Relationship Map turns scattered project data into an explainable network of relationships. It shows how each asset connects to evidence, risks, playbooks, suppliers, funders, finance models, governance approvals, MRV claims, trust badges, countries, Maqasid/Mizan principles and verified impact. This helps investors, governments, project owners and AI agents understand why a recommendation exists, what evidence supports it and what action should happen next.
