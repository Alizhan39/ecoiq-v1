EcoIQ Supplier & Funding Marketplace

From recommended action to funded implementation.

EcoIQ Supplier & Funding Marketplace connects industrial modernisation projects with qualified suppliers, finance routes and implementation partners. Once EcoIQ identifies a loss, harm or upgrade opportunity, the marketplace helps users find who can deliver the solution, how it can be financed and what evidence is required to verify impact.
