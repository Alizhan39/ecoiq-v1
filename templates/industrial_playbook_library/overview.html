EcoIQ Industrial Playbook Library

Reusable modernisation pathways for energy, industry, mining, agriculture and cities.

EcoIQ Industrial Playbooks turn repeated industrial problems into structured, evidence-based action plans. Each playbook explains what problem signs to look for, what data is required, which photos and documents prove the issue, which upgrades should be considered, how to estimate CAPEX/OPEX/payback, how to assess Maqasid/Mizan impact and how to verify results through MRV.
