EcoIQ Product Module

Omnimodal Evidence Panel

When AI reads, the interface shows what it sees.

EcoIQ combines documents, photos, videos, websites, sensor data and live inspection streams into one intelligent evidence panel. As agents read, watch and reason, the interface highlights sources, extracts metrics, builds visualisations and shows Maqasid/Mizan meaning in real time.
