EcoIQ Frontend Experience & Google Stitch Design System

A visual, mobile-first, Microsoft-ready interface for industrial intelligence.

EcoIQ Frontend Experience & Google Stitch Design System defines how EcoIQ should look, feel and behave across Command Centre dashboards, Asset Passports, Country Atlas maps, Knowledge Graphs, Mobile Inspection, Public Trust Portal, Data Room, MRV, Finance Engine and Microsoft-style enterprise workflows. It uses Google Stitch/Figma-style prototyping for UI concepts and production-ready frontend libraries for implementation.
