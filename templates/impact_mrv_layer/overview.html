EcoIQ Impact MRV Layer

Before, action, after — verified impact.

EcoIQ Impact MRV tracks the full evidence chain from baseline measurement to verified results. It captures photos, documents, bills, sensor data, invoices and monitoring signals before and after modernisation, then generates credible impact reports showing energy saved, emissions reduced, costs lowered, harm reduced and Maqasid/Mizan scores improved.
