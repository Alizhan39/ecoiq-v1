Measured vs Estimated

  • Measured: pipeline stage timings (wall-clock around the real work), stage counts, evidence/record counts, blocked-recommendation counts, human-review flags, and any token counts a model provider actually reported. A missing provider value is stored and shown as "not reported", never as zero.
  • Estimated (always labelled): every number in the generic-LLM baseline column of the comparison, and the three relative proxy indices (which weight measured counts by configurable factors).
  • Never produced: electricity (kWh), carbon (gCO₂e), or monetary figures. No defensible per-request measurement methodology exists for a mixed local/provider-hosted workload, so EcoIQ does not publish one.

Compute / Cost / Carbon Proxy Weights

Relative indices over real recorded work units: index = Σ count(unit) × weight(unit). Weights are deliberately coarse, visible, and configurable (settings.AI_OBSERVATORY_PROXY_WEIGHTS / AI_OBSERVATORY_COST_WEIGHTS); indices are only meaningful for comparing workloads under the SAME weights.

What EcoIQ Explicitly Does NOT Claim

  • No electricity, energy or carbon measurement — only relative, weight-based proxies.
  • No claim that any specific competing product behaves like the generic baseline — it is a documented reference construction.
  • No claim that disclosed confidence values are calibrated, or that historical evidence guarantees future outcomes.
  • No claim of automatic learning: retrieval reuses stored evidence; no model is retrained by any pipeline recorded here.
  • No scientific scale is implied by any percentage on the dashboard — each is the documented ratio above, nothing more.
