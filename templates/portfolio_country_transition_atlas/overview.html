EcoIQ Portfolio & Country Transition Atlas

Map transition risk, finance-ready projects and verified impact across countries and sectors.

EcoIQ Portfolio & Country Transition Atlas provides a geographic and portfolio-level view of industrial modernisation. It helps investors, governments, development banks, municipalities and industrial groups see where assets are located, where losses and harm are highest, where projects are ready for funding and where verified impact is already being delivered.
