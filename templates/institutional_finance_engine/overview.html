EcoIQ Institutional Finance Engine

From industrial evidence to investment-ready decisions.

EcoIQ Institutional Finance Engine converts asset evidence, playbooks, supplier quotes, funding routes and MRV assumptions into investor-grade financial models and decision memos. It helps users evaluate CAPEX, OPEX savings, payback, IRR, NPV, risk-adjusted return, grant fit, CSR fit, Islamic finance fit and Maqasid/Mizan impact before committing capital.
