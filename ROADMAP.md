## Vision

EcoIQ is an AI-native ethical operating system for industrial transition. It is not an ESG checkbox tool. It is not a sustainability consultancy. It is a global intelligence layer that makes the ethical and economic performance of companies legible — to investors, governments, regulators, and the public.

The platform is inspired by timeless principles of justice, stewardship, transparency, anti-corruption, and societal responsibility. These principles are universal. They belong to every ethical tradition, every economic system, every generation.

EcoIQ translates these principles into machine-readable, investor-ready intelligence.

### 2.3 — Scoring Architecture Evolution

**Current: 6-Pillar System (v1)**
```
PB×0.25 + Env×0.25 + Mod×0.20 + Trans×0.15 + AC×0.10 + EA×0.05 − HarmPenalty(≤30)
```

**Planned: 10-Dimension Intelligence Layer (v2)**

The 10 dimensions are organized into four intelligence clusters:

**Cluster 1: Societal Value**
- Justice & Fair Distribution (replaces Public Benefit, expanded)
- Community Benefit & Regional Development
- Human Dignity & Labor Standards

**Cluster 2: Environmental Stewardship**
- Pollution Reduction (formerly Environmental Responsibility)
- Water & Biodiversity Stewardship
- Climate Transition Readiness

**Cluster 3: Governance & Integrity**
- Transparency & Disclosure Quality
- Anti-Corruption & Procurement Integrity
- Industrial Responsibility (regulatory compliance, safety)

**Cluster 4: Strategic Resilience**
- Responsible Modernization (unchanged from v1)
- Long-Term Ethical Value Creation

**Three Synthetic Indices (v2):**
- **Profit Extraction Index (PEI)**: Ratio of profit extraction to public value creation; high PEI triggers harm penalty
- **Benefit Creation Index (BCI)**: Positive contribution to communities, environment, economy per dollar of revenue
- **Transition Readiness Score (TRS)**: How prepared is this company for the 2030–2050 industrial transition

**Harm Signal System (expanded):**
- Pollution severity (unchanged)
- Labour rights violations (new)
- Tax haven activity (new)
- Supply chain human rights risk (new)
- Regulatory sanctions in past 3 years (new)
- All harm signals are capped at 35 points total (up from 30)

### 2.4 — AI Video System

EcoIQ produces AI-generated intelligence videos autonomously. Companies do not commission or edit these videos.

**Video Types:**
1. **Path to 100** — What would it take for this company to score 100?
2. **Sector Benchmark** — How does this company compare to its sector?
3. **Country Intelligence Brief** — AI-narrated overview of a country's industrial transition
4. **Investor Readiness Profile** — For companies seeking ethical investment
5. **Harm Reduction Spotlight** — Focus on a specific harm signal and how to resolve it

**Production Pipeline:**
1. AI generates narration script (60–90 seconds)
2. AI generates Higgsfield scene prompt (visual + motion description)
3. Admin reviews and approves script
4. Higgsfield renders video
5. Admin pastes video URL
6. Status: `published`

**Automation target (Phase 3):** Direct Higgsfield API integration for fully automated video generation.

## Technical Architecture

### Stack (immutable constraints)
- **Framework**: Django 5.2 + Python 3.11
- **Database**: SQLite (dev) → PostgreSQL (prod via Render)
- **AI**: Anthropic Claude (claude-opus-4-5) via `anthropic==0.104.1`
- **Frontend**: Server-side Django templates + vanilla CSS
- **Hosting**: Render (auto-deploy from `main` branch)
- **CMS**: Wagtail (already integrated)
- **No**: Docker, Kubernetes, Redis, Celery, React, Next.js

### Key Design Principles
1. **Idempotent seeds** — All `seed_*` commands use `update_or_create`
2. **Scoring is computed, not edited** — Scores are calculated from sub-scores; never manually overridden via admin
3. **AI content has disclaimers** — Every AI field includes "EcoIQ AI-generated analysis" disclosure
4. **Harm penalty is transparent** — Shown in admin and public profile
5. **No vendor lock-in** — All data in PostgreSQL; exportable at any time
